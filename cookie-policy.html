Cookie Policy

Cookie Policy Content

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Our Approach to Cookies

Hollywood Coon uses no tracking cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other third-party tracking or advertising technology. Your browsing activity on our site is not monitored, recorded, or shared with anyone.

Service Worker Cache

Our Service Worker caches static assets for performance. When you visit hollywoodcoon.com, a Service Worker may store copies of HTML pages, CSS stylesheets, JavaScript files, fonts, and images in your browser’s cache storage. This allows pages to load faster on repeat visits and enables basic offline functionality. The Service Worker does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

How to Clear the Cache

You can clear this cache in browser settings at any time. In most browsers, navigate to Settings → Privacy & Security → Clear Browsing Data, and select “Cached images and files” or “Site data.” You can also unregister the Service Worker directly through your browser’s developer tools (Application → Service Workers → Unregister).

Third-Party Cookies

No third-party cookies are set by hollywoodcoon.com. We do not embed third-party widgets, social media plugins, or advertising scripts that would place cookies on your device. Our contact form is processed by Formspree, which does not set cookies on your browser.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our approach to cookies or caching, we will update this page and revise the “Last updated” date above. For questions about this policy, please contact us at hello@hollywoodcoon.com.
