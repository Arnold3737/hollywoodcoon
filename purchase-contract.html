Purchase Contract

Purchase Contract Terms

Last updated: April 10, 2026

1. Deposit

A $500 non-refundable deposit is required to reserve a kitten. The deposit is applied toward the final purchase price. Deposits are non-transferable between litters unless agreed upon in writing by Hollywood Coon.

2. Balance Due

The remaining balance is due in full before the kitten leaves the breeder. Payment must be received via approved methods (wire transfer, cashier’s check, or other pre-arranged method) prior to pickup or shipment.

3. Spay/Neuter Requirement

All pet kittens must be spayed or neutered by 18 months of age. Proof of spay/neuter from a licensed veterinarian must be provided to Hollywood Coon within 30 days of the procedure. Failure to comply voids the health guarantee.

4. Health Guarantee

Hollywood Coon provides a 2-year genetic health guarantee covering HCM (hypertrophic cardiomyopathy) and PKD1 (polycystic kidney disease). If a kitten is diagnosed with either condition by a board-certified veterinary cardiologist or internist within two years of purchase, a replacement kitten of equal value will be offered from a future litter.

5. Veterinary Examination Window

The buyer must have the kitten examined by a licensed veterinarian within 72 hours of taking possession. If the veterinarian identifies a significant health issue not disclosed by Hollywood Coon, the buyer may return the kitten for a full refund of the purchase price (excluding the deposit). After 72 hours, the health guarantee terms in Section 4 apply.

6. Return Policy

Hollywood Coon accepts kittens back at any age, for any reason, no questions asked. Kittens may not be resold, rehomed, or surrendered to a shelter. If at any point the buyer can no longer care for the cat, they must contact Hollywood Coon to arrange return. No refund is issued for returned cats beyond the 72-hour veterinary window.

7. TICA Registration

All kittens are sold with TICA (The International Cat Association) registration. Registration papers will be provided within 30 days of full payment and completion of the spay/neuter agreement (for pet kittens).

8. Last Updated

This contract was last updated on April 10, 2026. Hollywood Coon reserves the right to update these terms at any time. Buyers will be notified of any changes prior to finalizing a purchase.
