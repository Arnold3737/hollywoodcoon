Join Our Waiting List

Reserve your place for an upcoming litter of champion-lineage Maine Coon kittens

How It Works

Our waiting list ensures every family is matched with the perfect Maine Coon kitten. Here's how the process works from start to finish.

1

Submit Your Interest

Submit your interest form below. Tell us about your household, preferences for color or gender, and any questions you have.

2

We'll Be in Touch

We'll contact you within 48 hours to discuss your preferences and answer any questions about our program.

3

Secure Your Spot

Secure your spot with a $500 deposit (non-refundable, applied to kitten price). Your deposit reserves your place for the next available litter matching your preferences.

4

Meet Your Kitten

We match you with the perfect kitten from our next litter. You'll receive photos and updates as your kitten grows.

Deposit Terms

$500 non-refundable deposit. Applied toward final kitten price. Deposit reserves your place for the next available litter matching your preferences.

Ready to Join?

Contact us to get on the waiting list or ask any questions. Email hello@hollywoodcoon.com or call (424) 555-0198.
