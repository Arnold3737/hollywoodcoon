Health Testing

Every kitten leaves our cattery with a comprehensive health guarantee backed by veterinary testing

Our Health Testing Program

Health is the foundation of our breeding program. Every breeding cat is screened annually, and every kitten undergoes a full battery of tests before going home. We work with Dr. Smith, DVM, for cardiac evaluations and use Optimal Selection and UC Davis laboratories for DNA screening.

Tests We Perform

♥

HCM Echocardiogram

Cardiac ultrasound performed by Dr. Smith, DVM. Breeding cats are screened annually. This is the gold standard for detecting hypertrophic cardiomyopathy in Maine Coons.

⚖

PKD1 DNA Test

Polycystic kidney disease screening via Optimal Selection or UC Davis laboratory. DNA-based test ensures no carrier status is passed to offspring.

⚖

SMA DNA Test

Spinal muscular atrophy screening via Optimal Selection. This genetic test confirms our breeding cats are clear of the SMA gene mutation.

⚘

FIV/FeLV Blood Panel

Every kitten is tested for feline immunodeficiency virus and feline leukemia virus by blood panel before leaving our cattery.

⚗

Ringworm Culture & Fecal Panel

A ringworm culture and comprehensive fecal panel are performed to ensure each kitten is free from fungal infections and intestinal parasites.

⚛

Deworming & Vaccination

Every kitten receives deworming ×3 (Pyrantel + Fenbendazole) and FVRCP vaccine ×2 before going home. This protocol ensures strong immune protection from day one.
