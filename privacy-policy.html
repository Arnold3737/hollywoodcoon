Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy Content

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Hollywood Coon (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the website hollywoodcoon.com. This Privacy Policy describes what personal data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently.

Data Collection

We collect personal information only when you voluntarily provide it through our contact form or waiting list inquiry. The information we collect may include your name, email address, phone number, and the content of your message. We do not use Google Analytics or tracking pixels. No data is collected passively through cookies, scripts, or third-party trackers. Our site uses a Service Worker solely for caching static assets to improve page load performance — this does not collect or transmit any personal data.

How We Use Your Data

Contact form data is processed by Formspree, a GDPR-compliant form handling service. When you submit the contact form, your information is transmitted securely to Formspree, which forwards it to our email inbox. We use the information you provide solely to respond to your inquiry, process kitten reservations, manage waiting list requests, and communicate with you about our kittens and cattery. We do not use your data for marketing purposes unless you explicitly opt in. We do not build user profiles, run retargeting campaigns, or engage in behavioral advertising of any kind.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes. Your information is shared only with Formspree (for form processing) and, if applicable, with veterinary professionals or shipping partners solely to facilitate the delivery of your kitten. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of Hollywood Coon, our customers, or the public.

Cookies and Local Storage

Cookies are used only for essential site functionality via Service Worker cache. We do not set any tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies. The Service Worker caches HTML, CSS, JavaScript, fonts, and image files locally in your browser to speed up repeat visits. No personal information is stored in the cache. You can clear this cache at any time through your browser settings. See our Cookie Policy for more details.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. You may request a copy of your data, ask us to update inaccurate information, or request that we permanently delete your records from our systems. If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to restrict processing and the right to data portability. California residents have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what data is collected and the right to opt out of data sales (though we do not sell data). To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and correspondence for as long as necessary to fulfill your request and maintain records of kitten sales. Waiting list data is retained until your request is fulfilled or you ask to be removed. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Our website is served over HTTPS with strict transport security. Form submissions are encrypted in transit via TLS. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us at hello@hollywoodcoon.com or call (424) 555-0198. Our mailing address is Los Angeles, CA 90028.
