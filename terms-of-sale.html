Terms of Sale Content

Last updated: April 10, 2026

General Terms

These Terms of Sale govern the purchase of Maine Coon kittens from Hollywood Coon. By placing a deposit or completing a purchase, you agree to these terms and the conditions outlined in our Purchase Contract. All sales are final once the kitten has been transferred to the buyer, provided the buyer has fulfilled all conditions of the purchase contract, including the 72-hour veterinary examination requirement.

Payment Terms

A $500 non-refundable deposit is required to reserve a kitten. The remaining balance must be paid in full before the kitten is released. Accepted payment methods include wire transfer and cashier’s check. Personal checks are not accepted. Kitten prices range from $2,000 to $2,400 and are set at the time of deposit. Prices are subject to change for future litters but are locked once a deposit is placed.

Limitation of Liability

Hollywood Coon’s total liability arising from the sale of a kitten shall not exceed the purchase price paid by the buyer. Hollywood Coon is not liable for veterinary expenses, consequential damages, loss of companionship, or any indirect costs incurred by the buyer after the kitten has been transferred. The health guarantee detailed in our Purchase Contract represents the sole and exclusive remedy for genetic health conditions.

Dispute Resolution

In the event of a dispute, both parties agree to first attempt to resolve the matter through good-faith negotiation. If a resolution cannot be reached, disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration in Los Angeles County, California, in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

Governing Law

These Terms of Sale and the Purchase Contract are governed by the laws of the State of California, without regard to its conflict of laws provisions. Any legal proceedings shall be conducted in the courts of Los Angeles County, California. By purchasing a kitten from Hollywood Coon, you consent to the jurisdiction of these courts.
