Shipping & Delivery

Safe, comfortable delivery options for your new Maine Coon kitten anywhere in the United States

Delivery Options

We offer four ways to get your kitten home safely. Every option includes a health certificate, a travel carrier, and a comfort kit for the journey. Please note: a heat embargo is in effect June 15 – August 31 for air cargo shipments to protect your kitten during extreme temperatures.

How It Works

1

Local Pickup — Free

Pick up your kitten in person from our cattery in Los Angeles. We'll walk you through care instructions, send you home with a kitten kit, and make sure you're fully prepared. No additional cost.

2

Ground Transport — $150

Door-to-door ground delivery within 200 miles of Los Angeles. Your kitten travels in a climate-controlled vehicle with an experienced pet transporter. Includes a travel carrier and comfort kit.

3

Air Cargo — $300–$500

Airline cargo service to major US airports. Your kitten flies in a pressurized, temperature-controlled cabin. Price varies by destination. Heat embargo applies June 15 – August 31.

4

Flight Nanny — $500–$800

A trusted flight nanny accompanies your kitten in-cabin on a commercial flight to your nearest airport. The most personal and comfortable option. Your kitten is hand-delivered at the gate.

Pricing Summary

Method Range Cost
Local Pickup Los Angeles area Free
Ground Transport ≤ 200 miles from LA $150
Air Cargo Major US airports $300–$500
Flight Nanny Nationwide $500–$800

Heat embargo is in effect June 15 – August 31 for all air cargo shipments. Contact us to discuss the best delivery option for your location.
